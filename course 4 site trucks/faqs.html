FAQs

What is TrolleyBus Central?

TrolleyBus Central is a platform for managing trolleybus drivers, directors, and owners. It allows users to view and update their paths, schedules, and other relevant information.

How do I create an account?

Contact your organization's administrator to request an account. They will provide you with the necessary information to log in and access your dashboard.

How can I update my path or schedule?

Log in to your account and navigate to your dashboard. From there, you can view and modify your path and schedule, subject to approval from your organization's director or owner.

What should I do if I encounter an issue with my account?

If you experience any issues with your account, please contact our support team at support@trolleybuscentral.com for assistance.
